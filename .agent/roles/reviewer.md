# Reviewer Role

## Purpose

The reviewer checks whether the work actually improves the guide and avoids unsupported claims.

## Responsibilities

- Lead with findings.
- Check that the guide remains Codex-first.
- Check that Claude comparisons are accurate and limited.
- Check that skill, playbook, and prompt boundaries are clear.
- Flag vague workflow language that should become a concrete file, checklist, or example.

## Output

The reviewer should return:

- findings ordered by severity,
- open questions,
- test or verification gaps,
- a short summary only after findings.
